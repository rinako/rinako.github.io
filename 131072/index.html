HOW TO PLAY: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two identical tiles touch, they merge and become the next level of tile. Get a tile to the 17th level (131072) or much more higher scores!

The game progress will be automatically saved to the browser, or use buttons to save, load or restart the game.

玩法：使用方向键或手指移动方块，将相同数字合并成更大数字。游戏进度会自动保存，或使用按钮保存、加载或重置。
